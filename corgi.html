Welsh Pembroke Corgi

The Welsh Pembroke Corgi is a type of dog belonging to the herding breed. Corgis were bred to herd cows, goats, horses, or any other type of animal humans need help herding. These dogs are often born without tails and are low to the ground to avoid being stepped on or kicked by larger animals.

  • Corgis are medium-sized dogs, despite their short legs!
  • Corgis are about 10-12 inches tall and about 24 inches long
  • Male Corgis typically weight about 30 pounds while females are about 28 pounds.
  • A Corgi's life expectancy is 12-13 years.
